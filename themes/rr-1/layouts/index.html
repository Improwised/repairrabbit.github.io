Features

manage your repair business like a pro

Dashboard

Get an exclusive summary dashboard and see what’s going in your business.

Appointment

Anyone can create a new appointment at their convenient time from any of their devices, it could be mobile/desktop and at their nearest location.

Ticket

Ticket is your job sheet that contains everything for a particular repair.

Inventory tracking

Tracking of different products at different location is possible, get required quantity information all the time, so you can order new at earliest.

Customer dashboard

Customers can view their appointment and ticket progress online.

Employee

A-Z management of different store in a single system along with all employee leave/holidays.

We have a unique workflow to generate invoice from ticket. As well as you can generate invoices for accessories.
